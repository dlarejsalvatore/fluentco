Where technology, insights, and performance meet. We are Fluent.

Enabling advertisers to identify, win, and keep the best customers for their business.

Publicly traded on NASDAQ (FLNT).

Headquartered in NYC, with team members across North America and in Europe.

Partnering with 500+ clients, including Fortune 500 brands.

A company grounded in marketing innovation.

The Fluent story begins in 2001, when Matt Conlin and Ryan Schulke meet as students on campus at St. John’s University in NYC. Fueled by their shared entrepreneurial spirit, the duo co-founded Fluent in 2010 with a vision to change the digital marketing industry through smarter technology and better economics. As Fluent continues to grow and evolve, Matt and Ryan remain committed to providing even greater value to our customers, our consumers, our team, and our communities.

Meet Our Team

Alex Mandel

Chief Financial Officer

Alex Mandel

Chief Financial Officer

As Fluent’s resident numbers whiz, Alex works closely with senior company executives on strategic finance initiatives, corporate strategy, and general financial management. Alex brings a contagious energy and over twenty-two years of corporate finance experience to his role at Fluent. Prior to joining the team, Alex served as CFO of IAC Applications, and helped lead the resurgence of their online loan marketplace, Lending Tree. Holding a B.A. in Economics from Tufts University and an M.B.A. from Columbia Business School, Alex started his career as an investment banker before entering the world of digital marketing. When he’s not crunching numbers, you can find Alex rallying the team at our Town Hall meetings or spending quality time with his family.

Brian Hogan

President, Data Solutions

Brian Hogan

President, Data Solutions

As an entrepreneur at heart, Brian was drawn to Fluent’s spirit of innovation from day one. As President of Data Solutions , Brian helps the world’s leading brands leverage first-party consumer insights to build and execute winning omni channel marketing strategies. Since launching Fluent Audiences in 2018, Brian and his team have worked to establish Fluent as an industry leader in deterministic data. Before joining the team, he served as co-founder and COO of LifeScript, one of the largest digital health marketing platforms in the industry. Brian is a member of the DMA’s Council for Data Integrity and a graduate of The University of San Diego. As a Southern California native, Brian enjoys spending time at the beach with his family or coaching youth sports (and enthusiastically cheering on his two sons from the sidelines).

Dan Barsky

General Counsel

Dan Barsky

General Counsel

Affectionately known as “The Bulldog,” Dan oversees risk management and compliance at Fluent. Championing a consumer-first approach, he partners closely with the product and creative teams to ensure privacy-safe experiences across each of Fluent’s digital media properties. Though he hails from Main Line Philadelphia, Dan traveled up and down the East Coast for his education — earning his BBA from George Washington University, his law degree from University of Miami, and his LLM in tax law from New York University. Before joining Fluent, Dan worked for Snackable Media and Hanover Direct. In his spare time, Dan can be spotted biking to work from the Upper West Side, hitting the slopes at his favorite ski resort, or sipping tequila with his wife and two children on Fire Island.
